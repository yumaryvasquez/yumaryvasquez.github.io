 My current research focuses on the co-evolution of host-mircobe systems as it pertains to nutrition provisioning. I am using the leafhopper-Sulcia-Nasuia co-obligate system to ask questions on how microbes provide nutrition to their host, and in turn how hosts provide support for their microbes. Most of my research is computational using bioinformatic tools to perform genome comparative studies. If you are interested in learning more, check out the Bennett Lab website.
